About Ralpha Hospital and Maternity

Providing World-Class Care for Over a Decade

Our History

Founded in 2010, Ralpha Hospital and Maternity has been at the forefront of medical care in Eastern Nigeria. From our humble beginnings as a small clinic, we have grown into a comprehensive medical center, serving thousands of patients annually.

Our Commitment

At Ralpha Hospital, we are committed to providing the highest quality healthcare to our community. Our team of experienced doctors, nurses, and staff work tirelessly to ensure that every patient receives personalized, compassionate care.

We continuously invest in the latest medical technologies and training to offer our patients the best possible treatments and outcomes.

Our Facilities

Our state-of-the-art facilities include advanced diagnostic equipment, modern operating theaters, comfortable patient rooms, and a dedicated maternity ward. We also house the Tom and Chi Hospital and the Ralpha medical school, fostering the next generation of healthcare professionals.

Our Team

Our diverse team of medical professionals includes specialists in various fields, ensuring comprehensive care for all our patients. From general practitioners to specialized surgeons, our staff is dedicated to your health and well-being.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission: To provide exceptional healthcare services that improve the health and well-being of our community, delivered with compassion, integrity, and respect.

Vision: To be the leading healthcare provider in Eastern Nigeria, known for excellence in patient care, medical education, and innovative treatments.
