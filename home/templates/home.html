World-Class Care, Close to Home

For more than 10 years, Ralpha Hospital and Maternity has been a leader in the diagnosis and treatment of disease. Above all, we aim to provide the highest-quality health care and service to all our patients.

As the hospital affiliated with three branches, we are located in the heart of Eastern Nigeria, Abakaliki. The hospital includes the Tom and Chi Hospital and the Ralpha medical school.
